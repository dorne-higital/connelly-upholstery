A little about us

Who we are, and why we do it!

Connelly Upholstery is a family run business. The owner Michael has over 40 years experience in Upholstery. Time served in both Domestic and Business Upholstery including furniture repairs and Interiors, Michael has worked in several Upholstery businesses and also some large sofa chain stores gaining a raft of experience along the way. There isn't much Michael doesn't know about Upholstery!

The Finance and Operational side of the business is managed by Antonia. An Accountant by trade, Antonia's business experience alongside Michaels Upholstery expertise has enabled the business to grow over the last few years.

More recently Michaels youngest son has joined the team. He is oozing enthusiasm as he learns the trade.
